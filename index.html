How it works

Five steps to your first payout

01
Create your account
Sign up with email or your social account. Set your fitness preferences and we'll match you with the right challenges.
02
Choose a challenge
Browse solo, group, or 1v1 challenges. Filter by activity type, duration, entry fee, and prize pool size.
03
Pay your entry fee
Entry fees go directly into the prize pool. Stripe-powered payments keep everything secure and transparent.
04
Sync your activity
Connect your fitness tracker or wearable. Progress updates automatically and your rank adjusts in real time.
05
Collect your winnings
Winners confirm eligibility and receive their payout directly to their bank account. No delays, no gimmicks.

Challenge types

Pick your battleground

Solo

Prove it
to yourself

You versus a goal. Hit the target — miles, steps, workouts — before the clock runs out and claim your share of the prize pool.

  • Individual performance ranking
  • Multiple activity types supported
  • Tiered prizes for top finishers
  • Age-band groupings available
Group

Team up.
Cash out.

Form a crew or get assigned to a bracket. Pool your collective effort against other groups and split the winnings if your team dominates.

  • Team-based collective scoring
  • Automated group assignment
  • Per-group prize distributions
  • Private or public challenges
1v1

You vs.
one rival.

Call out a rival. Both put skin in the game. One winner takes everything. The most personal, most intense format on the platform.

  • Direct challenger / challenged setup
  • Optional custom entry fee
  • Winner-takes-all payout
  • Head-to-head leaderboard
